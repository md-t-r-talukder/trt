Admission Process

Step 1: Application

Complete and submit the online application form with required documents.

Step 2: Assessment

Students undergo an academic assessment and interview.

Step 3: Decision

Admission decision communicated within two weeks.

Step 4: Enrollment

Complete enrollment process and fee payment.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Previous academic records
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
